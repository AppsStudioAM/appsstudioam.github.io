Core Gameplay

How to Play

Simple to learn, impossible to put down. Drag blocks, fuse matches, and chase your highest score.

🧩

Drag, Drop & Fuse

Drag blocks from your queue and drop them on the grid. When matching numbers touch, they fuse into higher values with satisfying animations.

01
🔢

Merge to Score Big

Merge blocks to reach higher numbers and unlock massive combos. Strategic placement creates explosive chain reactions for huge point multipliers.

02
🎯

Strategic Chain Reactions

Plan your moves carefully. When a merge creates another adjacent match, chains fire off automatically with increasing multipliers and visual effects.

03
📐

Clean Grid Mechanics

A satisfying 5×5 grid with clean visuals and intuitive controls. Every cell, every block, every merge feels precise and responsive.

04
🖐️

One-Hand Portrait Play

Designed for comfortable one-handed gameplay in portrait mode. Play on the go — commute, break time, bed time — anywhere, anytime.

05
⚡

4 Power-ups

Use Bomb to remove blocks, Shuffle to refresh your queue, Upgrade to boost a block, or Undo to reverse a mistake — all earnable through gameplay.

Game Modes

5 Ways to Play

Whether you want intense time pressure or zen relaxation, there's a mode for every mood.

▶️

Classic Mode

Progress through increasingly challenging levels. Each level has a target score. Clear it to unlock the next world.

♾️

Endless Mode

No limits, no targets. Play until the board fills up. Push your score to the absolute maximum and chase your personal best.

⏱️

Time Attack

90 seconds on the clock. Race against time for the maximum score. Think fast, fuse faster — every second counts.

🧘

Zen Mode

No pressure, no timer, no game over. Pure relaxation. The board auto-clears when full so you can play forever.

📅

Daily Challenge

A new seeded puzzle every day. Build your streak for bonus rewards. Compare scores with the community. Don't break the chain!
