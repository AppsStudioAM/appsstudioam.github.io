Frequently Asked Questions

Are your games free to play?
Yes! All games are free to download and play with optional cosmetic purchases.
Do your games work offline?
Most games support offline play. Multiplayer features require internet.
What Android version is required?
Android 6.0+ supported. Android 10+ recommended for best experience.
How can I report a bug?
Use our Contact page, email us, or reach us on Discord.
Will you release games for iOS?
iOS versions are in our roadmap for the future. Stay tuned!
